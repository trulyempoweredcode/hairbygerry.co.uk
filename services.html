Colour, cuts & styling, done properly

Every appointment at Hair by Gerry is one-to-one, unhurried and built around you — from bold creative colour and soft balayage to sharp cuts and Mayfair-quality blow-dries, right here in East Dulwich.

Colour that turns heads — and stays that way

If you've ever wanted a colour that's truly yours, this is where we start. Pastels, vivids, ombré and bold all-over shades, mixed and placed by hand and designed to flatter your skin tone, your hair and your everyday. Every creative colour begins with a proper chat so we agree on the look before anything touches your hair.

  • Pastels, vivids and rainbow blends, mixed by hand
  • Ombré and bold all-over colour, built to last
  • Bespoke shades matched to you in consultation
  • Organic, mineral-based colour alongside Wella & L'Oréal

From price set at consultation — every creative colour is quoted to your hair. See the full price list.

Soft, sun-kissed dimension that grows out beautifully

Balayage and highlights are about light in the right places. Whether you want a barely-there brightening or a full head of foils, the placement is tailored so it suits your face and softens the regrowth line — meaning fewer trips back and a colour that still looks good months on. Tints and regrowth are kept fresh and natural-looking too.

  • Full-head, half-head and T-section highlights
  • Organic balayage for natural, low-maintenance dimension
  • Full-head and regrowth tints kept fresh and natural
  • Placement tailored to soften your regrowth line

From £95 (T-section). Half-head from £110, full-head from £120, organic balayage from £125. See the full price list.

A cut and finish that works the morning after, too

A great cut should suit how you actually wear your hair — not just look good for the photo at the basin. From a tidy trim to a full restyle, Gerry shapes the cut around your hair type and lifestyle, then finishes with a blow-dry honed in central London. Plenty of clients come in just for the blow-dry, and leave with a Mayfair finish without the trip to Mayfair.

  • Ladies' cut & blow dry, shaped to your hair
  • Full restyles for a fresh new direction
  • Mayfair-quality blow-dries, available on their own
  • Advanced cutting trained at Vidal Sassoon

From £63 (ladies' cut & blow dry; rough dry from £58.50). Restyles quoted higher at consultation. See the full price list.

Healthier, smoother hair you'll feel straight away

Colour and styling work best on hair that's looked after. Add a conditioning treatment to leave your hair softer and shinier, ask about Brazilian smoothing to calm frizz and cut down your styling time, or finish with a quick brow tidy. New colour clients are always offered a free patch test 48 hours before their first colour — a simple allergy check so your appointment is safe and stress-free.

  • Deep conditioning treatments for softer, shinier hair
  • Brazilian smoothing to tame frizz and speed up styling
  • Quick brow tidy to finish your look
  • Free patch test for new colour clients, 48 hours before

Conditioning treatments from £20. Smoothing quoted at consultation. See the full price list.
