Meet Gerry

The person behind the chair

Trained at L'Oréal in Rio and Vidal Sassoon, and in Advanced Colour Techniques with Alfaparf Milano, Gerry has spent years in busy London salons — Mad Lilly's in Hampstead and B-zar in Soho — before opening his own one-to-one studio in East Dulwich.

It means his full, unhurried attention from consultation to finish, organic and mineral-based colour that's kinder to your hair, and a result that looks like central London, right in your neighbourhood.

More About Gerry
The Studio

A space with real character

No rows of chairs, no rush. Gerry's studio sits inside Thai Vedic Massage on Crawthew Grove — a warm, colourful room with vintage Hollywood prints, plants and good coffee or herbal tea.

You get the whole appointment to yourself: a proper consultation, organic colour, and time to relax while the magic happens. A 10-minute walk from East Dulwich station, with free parking outside.
