Before Your First Colour

A quick step that keeps you safe

New colour clients: your free patch test

If it's your first colour appointment with Gerry, you'll need a quick, free patch test (an allergy alert) at least 48 hours beforehand. It takes moments — a tiny dab of colour behind the ear or inside the elbow — and it's how we make sure your colour service is comfortable and safe for you.

Just pop in or get in touch to arrange yours ahead of your appointment. There's no charge, and no obligation to book.
