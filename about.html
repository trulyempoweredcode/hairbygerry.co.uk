Meet Gerry

The colour specialist behind every appointment at the East Dulwich studio — one chair, one client, his full attention.

From Rio to East Dulwich

A central-London colourist, right on your doorstep

Gerry Hagemeyer trained in Hairdressing at L'Oréal in Rio de Janeiro and Barbering at Kensington and Chelsea College, and more recently in Advanced Cuts at Vidal Sassoon. He has worked as a Hair Stylist in Rio, Brazil and London for the past 7 years at various salons, including Mad Lilly's in Hampstead and B-zar in Soho.

In 2018 he brought all of that experience home, opening his own one-to-one studio inside Thai Vedic Massage in East Dulwich. No rush, no double-booking — just you in the chair and a colourist who genuinely listens, working with organic and mineral, ammonia-free colour alongside trusted Wella and L'Oréal lines.

The result is salon-quality colour and cutting in a calm, friendly space — the kind of finish you'd travel into town for, found a 10-minute walk from East Dulwich station.
